Welcome to GRP24 Theme Park

You are Welcome

We are delighted to see you on our page and would be privileged to welcome you in person. In our wonderful family park, you will enjoy all our different rides, attractions and facilities.

Our History

We are a newly built facility, a hidden gem of entertainment. It has started as a small park but has already reached the scale of having 5,000 happy visitors per day.

Why Choose Us

There are three themed pavilions with rides, a food court, secured covered territory, friendly and professional staff, and special events every weekend.

Rides and Facilities

We are happy to see you

Prepare to immerse yourself into a world of fun and thrill. You can ride on our carousels. Maybe you should try to find your way to our log ride section and ride away the day. If your interest is in vehicles, you will have a thrill riding our bumper cars.

Amazing fun for everyone

Feel free to spoil yourself at our food court if you are afraid of speed and height

Too tired after long week?

Stress is not for everyone. You are welcome to relax in our public park area with all necessary amenities in close proximity.

Too young for a ride?

We have a special kindergarten section area, where your small kids can enjoy a unique programme developed by our talented animators.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening times?

Our opening times from Mondays through Fridays is 9am to 6pm, 8am to 9pm on Saturdays and Sundays. We open everyday of the year except on the 29th of December to the 4th of January every year

Do you offer electric car parking?

There are Electric Car Charging Points in our ultra modern car park. You can ask a staff to point you in the right direction of buy a sitemap of our park for easy navigation.

Do you offer discounted tickets for families with young children?

We offer discounted tickets when you book online in advance. Children under 3 years old visit the theme park for free and all entry tickets include all rides. However tickets bought on site are not discounted.

Where can I purchase snacks and drinks at your park

There is a huge selection of stalls and boothes selling all types of food, snacks and drinks. You will definitely be spoiled for choice!

Can we bring our own food/picnic in to the park

We have benches around the theme park suitable for picnics. We also have special areas where young children can be safely fed. So feel free to pack a picnic basket.

What retail shops do you have at the park?

You can find a large selection of shops that sell a range of souvenirs from your favourite rides, clothing, snacks and drinks, toys and much much more!
