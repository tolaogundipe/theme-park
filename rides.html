There's Something For Everyone To Enjoy

OUR RIDES

Flying up in the Air

Humans can not fly. But in our park, you will get that feeling of being up in the air and flying high above the ground. "Flying up in the Air" is one of the most in-demand attractions of our park, and that will leave you breathless, carving for more thrill and fun.

Bumper Cars

You don't need a driving license to enjoy our bumper cars attraction. Drivers from age 5+ are welcome to test their driving skills. It is a classic attraction that never goes out of style. Our cars are two-seaters, which means couples may drive together and parents can accompany their kids.

Merry-Go-Rounds

The carousel is for our young visitors with parents. We have over 15 different types. Make memories for a lifetime and take them with you after your visit. Or dive into the past when you were a kid to refresh your emotions.

Roller Coasters

It is a type of ride everyone shall try in their life. Our Roller Coasters will win your heart and see you as a returning visitor every week. It may look scary from the outside, but when you are in, the fear goes away. We suggest you take photos during your ride to keep the moment with you forever.

OUR FACILITIES

First Aid Booth

Life is unpredictable. Our well-trained paramedics are always there if you seek medical help: first-aid box and CIRP devices are available upon the first request. Please follow the guidelines of our staff on the ground.

Accessible Rest Rooms

We are keeping our goals towards an inclusive environment. People with determination are most welcome to Group 24 Amusement park. Dedicated restrooms are located in designated locations.

24/7 ATM Machines

Short of cash? ATM's of the leading banks are located close to the ticket office and Carousel facilities so you never run out of cash. We accept payment by card and cash..

Free On-Site Parking

We provide free parking for all our visitors. The capacity of our parking lot is for 500 vehicles. Valet parking is available at the park and chargeable separately.

FOOD AND SHOPPING

Hot Breakfast Stalls

Our talented Chefs are waiting for you at breakfast stands. Healthy, vegan, kosher and halal food options are available. Start your journey in our park with fantastic breakfast options.

Snack Shops

A wide range of various snacks is available for you at the Park. They are always fresh, tasty and we suggest trying our signature Chicken Wrap.

Dessert Stations

Our distinctive and separate dessert station offers a selection of ice cream, cakes, cookies, candies and chocolate. Spoil yourself with it and enjoy your day in the park.

Souvenir Shop

You can find your favourite heroes and a mascot of our Park at the Souvenir Shop. Please stop for a second and buy something you can keep with you as a valuable memory of your perfect day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening times?

Our opening times from Mondays through Fridays is 9am to 6pm, 8am to 9pm on Saturdays and Sundays. We open everyday of the year except on the 29th of December to the 4th of January every year

Do you offer electric car parking?

There are Electric Car Charging Points in our ultra modern car park. You can ask a staff to point you in the right direction of buy a sitemap of our park for easy navigation.

Do you offer discounted tickets for families with young children?

We offer discounted tickets when you book online in advance. Children under 3 years old visit the theme park for free and all entry tickets include all rides. However tickets bought on site are not discounted.

Where can I purchase snacks and drinks at your park

There is a huge selection of stalls and boothes selling all types of food, snacks and drinks. You will definitely be spoiled for choice!

Can we bring our own food/picnic in to the park

We have benches around the theme park suitable for picnics. We also have special areas where young children can be safely fed. So feel free to pack a picnic basket.

What retail shops do you have at the park?

you can find a large selection of shops that sell a range of souvenirs from your favourite rides, clothing, snacks and drinks, toys and much much more!
